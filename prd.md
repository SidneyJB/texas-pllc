## 7) Operations Workflow

**A. Intake & Qualification (10 min)**  
Client completes gated form (profession, license #s, ownership, name choices, RA consent). Payment captured; timestamp logged.

**B. Pre-Filing (10 min)**  
Name availability screen → purpose language → eligibility check.

**C. Drafting (15 min)**  
Prepare Form 206, generate profession-specific Operating Agreement, bank resolutions.

**D. Filing (≤ 48 hr)**  
Submit Form 206 via SOSDirect; record timestamp; send filing confirmation.

**E. EIN & Packet Delivery (≤ 48 hr)**  
Obtain EIN; deliver EIN letter, OA, RA info, and compliance checklist.

**F. Add-ons (if purchased)**  
Submit TMB/board applications and track progress.

**G. Close-out**  
Final delivery email + RA renewal lifecycle (D-300, D-60, D-30 notifications).
