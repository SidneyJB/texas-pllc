# Product Context: Texas PLLC Formation Service

## Why This Project Exists

### Problem Statement
Professionals forming Texas PLLCs face:
- **Confusion:** PLLC vs LLC differences unclear
- **Fear of mistakes:** Complex legal requirements and compliance concerns
- **Limited options:** Generic formation services lack profession-specific expertise
- **Hidden costs:** Unclear pricing with surprise upsells
- **Slow service:** Long wait times for filing completion

### Customer Jobs-To-Be-Done

| Persona | Primary Job | Frictions |
|---------|------------|-----------|
| **Therapist/Counselor** | "Form my Texas PLLC fast and correctly so I can start seeing clients." | Confusion about PLLC vs LLC; fear of mistakes; limited budget |
| **Physician or PA Group** | "Stay compliant and optimize tax treatment for my practice." | PLLC vs PA confusion; multi-owner complexity; liability concerns |
| **Engineer (PE)** | "Meet TBPELS rules and launch my firm with credibility." | Board filing sequence; liability protection; professional optics |
| **Attorney** | "Ensure my firm complies with State Bar and ethics rules." | Advertising rules, IOLTA setup, malpractice exposure |

## How It Should Work

### Service Flow
1. **Landing Page:** Profession-specific page with clear value prop
2. **Checkout:** Embedded spiffy.co form captures order and payment
3. **Intake:** Client provides profession, license #s, ownership, name choices, RA consent
4. **Pre-Filing:** Name availability check, purpose language, eligibility verification
5. **Drafting:** Form 206 preparation, profession-specific Operating Agreement, bank resolutions
6. **Filing:** Submit to Texas SOS within 48 hours
7. **EIN & Delivery:** Obtain EIN, deliver complete packet
8. **Add-ons:** Process board filings if purchased
9. **Close-out:** Final delivery + RA renewal lifecycle management

### Service-Level Agreement
- **Promise:** All complete orders filed within 48 business hours
- **Penalty:** $100 credit automatically issued if SLA missed
- **Clock starts when:** Intake complete, RA consent obtained, name availability confirmed
- **Note:** SOS outages don't pause clock (credit still issued)

## User Experience Goals

### Landing Page Experience
- **10-section blueprint:**
  1. Hero with star rating + guarantee seal
  2. Problem empathy (3-5 pain points)
  3. Solution clarity ("We handle everything")
  4. Benefits (3-7 outcomes)
  5. How It Works (3-step visual)
  6. Social Proof (testimonials, ratings)
  7. Pricing Table (Good-Better-Best, $749 emphasized)
  8. Guarantee (60-day money-back)
  9. FAQ (10-12 objections)
  10. Final CTA

### Copy Style
- 5th-7th grade reading level
- 3× "you" vs "we" ratio
- Benefit-led language
- Simple CTAs: "Get Started," "Claim My 48-Hour Filing"

### Checkout Experience
- **Target:** ≤ 6 minutes checkout time
- **Abandonment goal:** ≤ 30%
- Embedded spiffy.co form for seamless payment
- Clear pricing display with no hidden fees
- Trust signals throughout (guarantees, testimonials)

## Success Metrics
- Landing page conversion ≥ 10%
- Checkout time ≤ 6 min
- Checkout abandonment ≤ 30%
- On-time filing ≥ 95%
- Refund rate ≤ 3%
- Blended CPA ≤ $150 (Month 2), ≤ $120 (Month 3)
